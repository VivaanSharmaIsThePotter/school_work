The Most Popular F1 Cars


2 of the Most Popular Formula 1 Cars are -


The Mercedes-AMG F1 W11 EQ Performance, used in the 2020 Formula One Season and driven by Lewis Hamilton and Valterri Bottas. It won Mercedes their 7th consecutive World Constructors' Title and won Lewis Hamilton his 7th World Drivers' Title.



The McLaren-Honda MP4/4, used in the 1988 Formula One Season and driven by Ayrton Senna and Alain Prost. It won McLaren their 4th World Constructors' Title, and first with their partnership with Honda, and won Ayrton Senna his 1st World Drivers' Title.
